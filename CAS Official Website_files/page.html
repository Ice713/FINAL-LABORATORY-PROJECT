𝐉𝐔𝐒𝐓 𝐈𝐍: College of Arts and Sciences proclaimed as the overall 2nd placer in the recently concluded Pluma 2022 lead by the NVSU Varsity courier.

After its hope...ful win last Charter Anniversary, the College of Arts and Sciences once again brought home another trophy as the overall second placer of Pluma 2022. Further proving their talents and skills in arts and writing.

Congratulations to all our CASmates who swept six places on various categories;

1st Placer: Julie Halaman (News Writing English)
1st Placer: Christian Luis Shih (Editorial Cartooning English)

2nd placer: Aira Sofia Almuete (Photojournalism English)
2nd placer: Khayenne Ganggangan (Feature Writing English)

3rd Placer: Shairine Rose Aduca (Photojournalism Filipino)
3rd Placer: Julie Halaman (Sports Writing English)

As well as our very brave participants:

Cherry Ann Anchos - Feature Writing Filipino
Precious Niña Bayatan - Feature Writing Filipino
Jyvann Pascual - Sports Writing

Your CASSC family is very proud of what you have achieved. We are hoping that this win will spark the new era for the yellow tigers, and inspire more of our CASmates to join us in the future! Thank you very much for roaring for us! KUDOS! 🐯
